I have been awarded multiple prizes for both my teaching and research. 

I have received the LSE Department of Economics Excellence in Teaching Award in 2020-21, and has also received the Highly Commended LSE Class Teacher Award for 2022. 

In recognition of my research, I have received the European Finance Association (EFA) Best Paper Award in Responsible Finance for my co-authored paper Green Asset Pricing. 

I have been also been recently selected as a finalist for the 2022 QCGBF Young Economist Prize.
